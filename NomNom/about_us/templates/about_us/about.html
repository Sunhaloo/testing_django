ABOUT US

OUR CRAFT

At Nomnom Express, we believe that great baking starts with simple, wholesome ingredients and a touch of care. From hand-kneading our dough to perfectly timing each bake, every step of our process is guided by passion and precision. Whether it’s a warm loaf, a delicate pastry, or a celebratory cake, we put our heart into creating treats that bring joy to every bite.

OUR PROMISE

We promise to deliver freshness and authenticity in everything we bake. By staying true to natural ingredients and artisanal methods passed down for generations, Nomnom Express ensures that every product reflects our dedication to quality, care, and tradition.

OUR BRAND STORY

For over a century, Nomnom Express has been more than just a bakery, it’s a part of Mauritius’ culinary story. Rooted in family tradition and fueled by innovation, our journey reflects the love of sharing fresh, handcrafted goodness with every household on the island.
